Frequently asked questions

If your question is not answered here, our team responds to every enquiry within 24 hours.

What Amazon fulfillment services do you offer from China?
We offer FBA consolidation (store in Shenzhen, drip-feed replenishments to Amazon), FBA prep (FNSKU labelling, poly bagging, bubble wrap, bundling, carton labels), FBM order fulfillment (pick, pack, and ship individual orders to Amazon buyers), multi-marketplace shipping (US, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Japan, Canada, Australia), and quality control inspection before any shipment leaves the warehouse.
How much does Amazon fulfillment from China cost?
Storage is $0.49 per CBM per day ($14.70 per month). Pick and pack is $0.99 per carton for FBA or per order for FBM. FNSKU labelling starts at $0.15 per unit. Poly bagging from $0.30 per unit. Receiving fees are zero. First 30 days of storage are free for new customers. No monthly subscription. No minimum volumes.
Which Amazon marketplaces do you ship to?
We ship to all active Amazon FBA marketplaces worldwide: United States, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Japan, Canada, and Australia. We also ship FBM orders and D2C orders to 220+ countries via DHL, FedEx, UPS, and economy carriers.
Can you handle both FBA and FBM from the same warehouse?
Yes. The same Shenzhen warehouse stores your inventory and fulfills both FBA bulk shipments to Amazon and individual FBM orders shipped directly to your customers. You can allocate inventory between FBA and FBM dynamically based on demand without maintaining separate stock pools.
What is FBA consolidation and how does it save money?
FBA consolidation means storing the bulk of your inventory in Shenzhen at $0.49/CBM/day instead of Amazon's rates ($27.55 to $84.76/CBM/month). You send small weekly replenishments to Amazon, keeping 2-4 weeks of stock at FBA at any time. This reduces storage fees by up to 83% during Q4 and improves your Inventory Performance Index score.
Do you handle FBA prep since Amazon discontinued it in 2026?
Yes. Amazon ended all FBA prep and labelling services on January 1, 2026. We handle everything Amazon used to do: FNSKU barcode application, poly bagging with suffocation warnings, bubble wrapping, bundling, carton labelling, and pallet building. Every unit is prepped to Amazon's current specification and barcode-verified before dispatch.
How does FBM fulfillment from China work?
When a customer buys your product on Amazon, the order syncs to our warehouse. We pick the item, pack it in your branded or standard packaging, and ship it directly to the buyer via DHL, FedEx, UPS, or economy carriers. Tracking uploads to your Seller Central. Delivery to US customers takes 3-5 business days via express.
Can my Chinese suppliers deliver directly to your warehouse?
Yes. Any factory in China can deliver to our Shenzhen warehouse. We receive, count every item against your purchase order, inspect for visible defects, photograph discrepancies, and notify you within 24 hours. Zero receiving fees on every delivery, regardless of volume or frequency.
