Kitting & Assembly

Multiple items from multiple suppliers, assembled into one ready-to-ship kit

Kitting is assembling individual items into a set that ships as a single unit. Each finished kit gets its own SKU so it can be tracked and managed as one product in your inventory. Building kits in bulk is faster and cheaper per unit than assembling them one at a time as orders arrive.

📦

Receive components from any number of suppliers

Different products from different factories all deliver to our Shenzhen warehouse. We receive, count and QC each delivery separately. Zero receiving fees regardless of how many suppliers are involved.

📋

Assemble kits to your exact specification

Your kitting instruction sheet tells us what goes in each kit. We position all components together on the assembly line, build each kit, apply your branded packaging, and seal it. Every kit gets a QC check before storage.

🏷

Assign a unique SKU to each finished kit

The assembled kit becomes a single trackable product in your inventory. One SKU, one barcode, one pick-and-pack step at dispatch. This reduces fulfilment time per order and cuts picking errors.

🚚

Store or ship immediately

Finished kits go into storage at $0.49/CBM/day (first 30 days free) until you're ready to ship. Or dispatch immediately to customers, Amazon FBA, or any destination worldwide.

Frequently Asked

Questions about branded packaging and kitting from China

What is the minimum order for custom branded packaging?
Production runs start from 50 pieces. This makes it practical to test a packaging design, run a limited edition, or start small before scaling up. Custom branded packaging can be printed and delivered to our Shenzhen fulfillment centre in as little as 10 days.
Is there an extra charge for using branded packaging?
No. Our standard $0.99 per order pick and pack rate applies whether we pack in your custom branded materials or in plain packaging. There is no surcharge for applying your branded boxes, poly mailers, tissue paper, inserts, stickers or tape.
What types of custom packaging can you produce?
Custom printed boxes, branded poly mailers, tissue paper with your logo or pattern, branded packing tape, thank-you cards, promotional inserts, sticker sheets, and custom gift wrapping. We work with local print suppliers in Shenzhen for fast turnaround and competitive pricing.
What is kitting and how does it work?
Kitting is assembling individual items from your inventory into ready-to-ship sets. Each kit gets assigned a unique SKU so it can be tracked and managed as a single unit in your inventory system. Building kits in bulk is more efficient because all components are positioned together on the assembly line, which reduces per-kit assembly time compared to building kits one at a time as orders come in.
What kind of products do you kit?
We kit for ecommerce product bundles, subscription boxes, crowdfunding reward tiers, promotional gift sets, Amazon multi-packs, and seasonal variety packs. If your kit involves combining items from multiple suppliers, we receive all components at our Shenzhen warehouse and assemble them into finished kits ready for storage or immediate dispatch.
Can you receive components from multiple suppliers for kitting?
Yes. Multiple suppliers can deliver different components to our Shenzhen warehouse. We receive, count and QC each delivery separately, then combine them into finished kits according to your specification. Zero receiving fees regardless of how many suppliers deliver to us or how often.
Do you handle subscription box fulfillment?
Yes. We kit subscription boxes on your schedule, with custom packaging and branded inserts applied to every box. As your subscriber base grows, the kitting operation scales with it. Each month's box can have a different product mix without disrupting the fulfillment workflow.
How much does kitting cost?
Kitting is priced based on the number of components per kit and the complexity of assembly. Simple 2-3 item kits start from $0.50 per unit. More complex assemblies with custom packaging, inserts and multiple components are quoted individually. Storage is $0.49 per CBM per day with the first 30 days free. Contact us with your kit specification for an exact quote.
