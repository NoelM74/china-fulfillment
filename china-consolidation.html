The supply chain story most D2C founders don't talk about publicly

You've built real demand. Customers love the product. But behind the scenes, the logistics is a mess — and it's costing you more than you realise.

  • 📦

    You're managing 4, 6, maybe 8 separate supplier relationships in China

    That's 4–8 sets of tracking numbers, freight quotes, customs filings, and WhatsApp threads. Each one takes time. Each one is a potential failure point. And as you add SKUs, the complexity compounds — until managing logistics becomes your full-time job instead of growing your brand.

  • 😤

    You've had a quality disaster — and found out when customers did

    The wrong colour. A broken component. Packaging that doesn't match your spec. By the time your customers flag it, it's already on 200 doorsteps and spreading across your reviews. A QC inspection in China costs a fraction of what one bad batch costs your brand.

  • 💸

    Your capital is tied up in inventory sitting in an expensive destination warehouse

    You sent 6 months of stock to a US 3PL because the freight economics demanded it. Now £40,000 of inventory is just sitting there — and the 3PL is billing you storage, receiving fees, and pick & pack on top of what you already paid to ship it there. Meanwhile, you can't react when a new product needs funding.

  • 🌍

    You're stuck in 1 or 2 markets because international shipping is too hard to set up

    You have customers in Australia, Canada, and Germany who want to buy. But your current setup makes shipping there either too expensive, too slow, or too complicated to scale. So you leave that revenue on the table — and your competitors don't.

  • 📉

    You're shipping big, infrequent batches — which means constant cash flow stress

    A $40,000 container shipment every quarter means 3 months of anxiety about whether it clears customs, arrives on time, and doesn't get lost. One delayed shipment can cause a stockout that tanks your rankings and your momentum at the worst possible time.

If you recognised yourself in even two of those scenarios, your logistics setup is quietly capping your growth — and we can fix it. Fast.

FAQ

Questions founders ask before getting started

I only have 2 suppliers right now. Is consolidation worth it at that size?
Yes — and now is the perfect time to set it up. Most brands add suppliers as they grow. Getting your consolidation infrastructure in place with 2 suppliers means you're ready to scale to 5 or 10 without any disruption. You'll also benefit immediately from lower storage costs, QC at origin, and simplified shipping — even from day one.
How does the 30-day free storage offer work?
Simple. When your first goods arrive in our Shenzhen warehouse, your 30-day free storage period begins. You pay nothing for storage during that time. After 30 days, the standard rate of $0.49 per CBM per day applies. No credit card required to start, no catch, no auto-enrolment into a contract.
Can you handle branded packaging, custom inserts, and kitting?
Absolutely — this is one of our core strengths for D2C brands. Custom boxes, tissue paper, thank-you cards, stickers, bundle assembly, subscription box kitting — we execute to your spec. Share your packaging requirements and we'll confirm feasibility and pricing before you commit.
How does Shopify integration actually work in practice?
We connect your Shopify store to our WMS via integration. New orders are synced automatically and dispatched without you doing anything. Inventory levels update in real time so your store never shows incorrect stock. Customers receive tracking notifications directly. For non-standard setups, our technical team can build a custom integration via API.
What happens if you find quality issues during inspection?
We flag the issue to you immediately with photos and a detailed report. At that point, the goods are still in China — meaning you still have full leverage with your supplier to demand rework, replacement, or a credit. This is the entire value of in-China QC: catching problems while fixing them is still free, not after they've shipped to 200 customers.
Do you offer volume discounts for high-growth brands?
Yes. We offer tiered pricing across storage, pick & pack, and shipping for brands hitting meaningful monthly volumes. The more you move through us, the lower your per-unit cost. Contact us with your approximate monthly volume and destinations and we'll put together a custom quote.
