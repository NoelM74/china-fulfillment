The Reality After Funding

Five things that wreck crowdfunding campaigns after the money's in the bank

Most campaigns fail at fulfillment, not funding. Here's where the damage happens.

  • 💸

    Shipping costs eat 20% of your budget before you notice

    Fulfillment is the most underestimated line item in crowdfunding. Manufacturing + freight + duties + 3PL fees + packaging + carrier costs. Add them up and your per-unit margin looks very different from your campaign spreadsheet. Most creators discover this after funding closes.

  • 🚢

    US 3PLs charge you to receive your own inventory

    Your container arrives from China. Your US 3PL charges $25-75 per pallet just to unload it. Then storage fees. Then pick and pack at $2-4 per order. Then the carrier bill. You've spent four figures before a single backer receives anything.

  • 🌍

    40 countries means 40 customs headaches

    Your US backers get their rewards. Your EU backers get a customs charge at the door and an angry email to you. Your Australian backers are still waiting. Crowdfunding fulfillment is a global logistics problem that most domestic 3PLs are not built for.

  • 📦

    Kitting 5 reward tiers with 3 add-ons is harder than it sounds

    Early Bird gets the base product. Standard gets the base plus a carry case. Deluxe gets everything plus 2 accessories and a signed postcard. Now multiply by 3,000 backers with different addresses, different add-ons, and a shipping deadline your factory is about to miss.

  • ⏳

    Your backers don't understand supply chain delays. They just see "late."

    The comment section turns hostile fast. Every week without a shipping update costs you future customers. The single biggest thing you can control is how quickly rewards move from your factory to their front door. The fewer steps in between, the fewer places for delays to stack up.

Frequently Asked

Questions campaign creators ask before choosing a crowdfunding fulfillment partner

How does crowdfunding fulfillment from China actually work?
Your manufacturer delivers finished rewards directly to our Shenzhen warehouse. We receive, count, QC inspect, then kit and pack each backer's order according to their reward tier. Orders ship to backers in 200+ countries with full tracking. Because your products are already manufactured in China, there's no container shipment to a US warehouse first. Rewards go straight from factory to backer.
Can you handle different reward tiers and add-ons?
Yes. We set up separate kitting configurations for each reward tier: Early Bird, Standard, Deluxe, add-on bundles, whatever your campaign structure requires. Each tier gets its own pick list and QC checklist. We've handled campaigns with 12+ different configurations shipping to 40+ countries at the same time.
Do you work with BackerKit and other pledge managers?
Yes. We accept CSV order exports from BackerKit, PledgeManager, PledgeBox, and direct exports from both Kickstarter and Indiegogo. Once you send us the export file, we import it into our system and begin processing within 24 hours.
What about customs duties? Will my backers get surprise charges?
DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) shipping is available on major routes including the US, UK, EU and Australia. Duties are settled before delivery so backers receive their rewards with no unexpected charges. This alone prevents the angry comments and one-star reviews that damage campaign ratings and kill repeat purchase potential.
How much does crowdfunding fulfillment from China cost?
Pick and pack is $0.99 per order. Storage is $0.49 per CBM per day. Receiving fees are always zero. No setup fees, no account fees, no minimum volume commitments. First 30 days of storage are free. Because we ship direct from China, you also eliminate the US 3PL receiving fees, domestic freight costs and secondary storage charges that typically add $2-4 per unit when fulfilling from a US warehouse.
Can you handle the surge when my campaign ships?
This is what we do. We've been processing high-volume batch shipments for 15 years. When your rewards arrive from the factory, we begin processing within 24 hours. Most campaigns with under 5,000 backers complete their entire fulfilment run within 2-3 weeks of inventory arrival.
What if my manufacturer is not in Shenzhen?
Your manufacturer can be anywhere in China. Domestic freight to our Shenzhen warehouse is cheap and fast. Most factories deliver to us within 2-3 days. In many cases, delivering to us is actually cheaper for your manufacturer than FOB port delivery because we're close to major domestic freight hubs.
My rewards include lithium batteries. Can you ship those?
Yes. We hold the required dangerous goods certifications for shipping lithium-ion and lithium-polymer batteries by air and sea. Many crowdfunding campaigns involve tech products with batteries, and we handle the MSDS documentation, UN38.3 test reports and carrier compliance so your shipments clear without delays or rejections.
