Frequently Asked

Questions sellers ask before launching COD from China

What is COD fulfillment from China?
Cash on delivery fulfillment from China means your products are stored in our Shenzhen warehouse and shipped to customers who pay the courier in cash (or mobile wallet, or POS card) when the package arrives at their door. We handle warehousing, picking, packing, and shipping. Local courier partners collect payment and settle funds back to your account on a weekly cycle.
Which countries do you offer COD shipping to?
We currently offer COD fulfillment to Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam, the Philippines, Hong Kong, and Taiwan in Southeast Asia, plus Saudi Arabia and the UAE in the Middle East. Coverage is expanding. Contact us for the latest country list and carrier options per market.
How does the COD payment settlement work?
When your customer pays the courier on delivery, the local carrier partner collects and pools the payments. We settle collected funds to your account on a weekly cycle, with flexible settlement options available for high-volume merchants. Online account statements let you track every COD collection in real time.
What happens if a customer refuses a COD package?
Refused packages (return to origin) are a normal part of COD commerce. We track RTO rates per country and per product category so you can adjust your ad targeting and product selection. Refused packages return to our warehouse for restocking or re-dispatch. Typical RTO rates in Southeast Asia range from 8-15% depending on market and product type. The data we provide helps you bring that number down over time.
How much does COD fulfillment from China cost?
COD service fees are based on destination country, package weight, and COD collection amount. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific product and market mix. Storage is $0.49 per CBM per day with the first 30 days free. There are no receiving fees and no minimum volume requirements.
Can I run COD alongside prepaid orders from the same warehouse?
Yes. Many of our merchants run COD for Southeast Asia and the Middle East while simultaneously fulfilling prepaid Shopify orders to the US and FBA replenishments to Amazon. One warehouse, one stock pool, multiple sales channels. Your inventory doesn't need to be split across different locations.
What payment methods do your COD couriers accept?
Depending on the destination country, our local courier partners accept cash, POS card payments, and mobile wallet payments (GCash in the Philippines, GoPay in Indonesia, MoMo in Vietnam, PromptPay in Thailand, STC Pay in Saudi Arabia, and others). Offering multiple payment options at delivery reduces refusal rates and increases successful collection.
Is COD fulfillment suitable for dropshipping from China?
Yes. COD is one of the most common models for China-to-Southeast-Asia dropshipping. You run ads, customers place orders, we pick and ship COD from Shenzhen. The courier collects payment on delivery. Many of our COD merchants started as dropshippers and scaled into branded operations using the same warehouse and the same COD infrastructure.
