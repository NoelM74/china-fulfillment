The Problem with Traditional Dropshipping

Most dropshippers lose customers before the product even arrives

If you're shipping from AliExpress or direct from a Chinese supplier with no warehouse in between, these problems are costing you money every day.

⏱

15-30 day shipping times

Your customer orders on Monday and waits three weeks. By then they've filed a chargeback, left a negative review, or bought from a competitor. Long shipping times are the single biggest cause of refund requests in dropshipping.

🚫

Quality you can't control

You've never seen the product in person. Supplier photos looked fine, but what arrives at your customer's door is wrong colour, wrong size, or broken. Returns cost you money and trust. Without inspection at the source, every order is a gamble.

👤

Suppliers who stop responding

Everything works until there's a problem. Then your supplier goes quiet. Out-of-stock items you're still selling. Delayed shipments with no tracking updates. No way to resolve customer complaints because you can't get a response from the person who has the inventory.

Frequently Asked

Questions about dropshipping fulfillment from China

How fast is shipping with your dropshipping service?
Express shipping via DHL, FedEx and UPS delivers to most countries in 3-7 days. Economy options are also available for 7-15 day delivery at lower cost. We process orders within 24 hours. Your customers get tracking numbers automatically.
What platforms do you integrate with for dropshipping?
We integrate with Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon, eBay, and Etsy. Orders sync automatically from your store to our warehouse. We also support automation tools like AutoDS and DSers. API access is available for custom integrations.
Can you source products for me from Chinese manufacturers?
Yes. Tell us what you want to sell and we source it from verified manufacturers in China. We negotiate pricing, order samples for your approval, inspect quality, and stock approved products in our Shenzhen warehouse. If the product doesn't exist from current manufacturers, we can arrange custom production at partner factories.
Do I need to buy inventory upfront?
It depends on your model. For traditional zero-inventory dropshipping, we can ship individual orders direct from supplier stock. For faster delivery and quality control, you stock inventory in our warehouse and we ship from your verified stock. Most sellers who scale past their first few months move to the warehoused model because it gives them control over delivery speed and product quality.
How much does dropshipping fulfillment cost?
Pick and pack is $0.99 per order. Storage is $0.49 per CBM per day with the first 30 days free. Shipping costs vary by destination, weight, and carrier. Express shipping via DHL, FedEx or UPS starts from $3/kg to the US. Zero receiving fees. No minimum order quantities.
Do you offer branded packaging for dropshipping orders?
Yes. Custom branded boxes, poly mailers, tissue paper, thank-you cards, and branded tape can be applied to every outbound order at the standard $0.99 pick and pack rate with no surcharge. Production runs for custom packaging start from 50 pieces.
How do you handle quality control?
Every batch is inspected when it arrives from your supplier. Units counted against your purchase order, spot-checked for defects, issues documented with photos. For outbound orders, we verify each pick before packing. This catches problems in Shenzhen where they cost pennies to fix, not at your customer's door where they cost a refund and a review.
What happens with returns and lost packages?
For lost packages, we handle carrier claims on your behalf. Every shipment is insured. For customer returns, we receive returned items at our Shenzhen warehouse, inspect them, and restock or dispose per your instructions. Your dedicated account manager coordinates issue resolution.
