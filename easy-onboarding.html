What You Need

To start, we need four things from you

Everything else — warehouse setup, system configuration, shipping account creation — we handle on our side.

1. Your product details

SKU list, product dimensions, weight per unit, and any special handling requirements (fragile, batteries, temperature sensitive). Photos help. A spec sheet is even better.

2. Your target markets

Where your customers are. Which Amazon marketplaces you sell on. Which countries you ship DTC orders to. This determines carrier selection and shipping rate quotes.

3. Your store or platform

The ecommerce platform your orders come from. Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon Seller Central, eBay, Etsy, or your custom platform. We configure the integration during onboarding.

4. Your supplier contact

So we can coordinate the first inbound delivery. Your supplier ships to our Shenzhen warehouse address. We can also arrange collection from your supplier if needed.

Onboarding FAQ

Questions new clients ask before getting started

How long does onboarding take?
Most new clients are operational within 3-5 business days. This includes account setup, store connection, and receiving your first inventory delivery. If your supplier is ready to ship, you can have products in our warehouse and orders flowing within one week.
Are there any setup fees or contracts?
No setup fees, no account fees, no lock-in contracts, and no minimum volumes. Our service runs on a pay-as-you-go basis. You top up your account and pay for services as you use them. If you stop using the service, there are no cancellation fees.
What ecommerce platforms can I connect?
We integrate with Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, Amazon, eBay, and most other major ecommerce platforms. If your platform is not directly supported, we can integrate via API or you can upload orders via our batch file template in CSV, XML, or XLS format.
What happens when my inventory arrives at your warehouse?
We scan every item for a 100% count check. We email you to confirm your products have arrived with the confirmed count and photos. Products are placed on bar-coded shelves in our warehouse within 24 hours of receipt, or up to 48 hours during busy periods. Zero receiving fees on every delivery.
How quickly are orders shipped?
All orders received before 4pm Hong Kong time ship the same day. Orders are picked from bar-coded shelves, packed according to your instructions (including branded packaging and inserts if applicable), and dispatched with tracking. We guarantee 100% pick and pack accuracy.
Do I get a dedicated account manager?
Yes. Every client is assigned a dedicated account manager during onboarding. They walk you through the setup process, help configure your store integration, coordinate your first inbound shipment, and remain your point of contact for ongoing support.
Can my supplier deliver directly to your warehouse?
Yes. Your supplier can ship directly to our Shenzhen warehouse address. We can also arrange collection from your supplier in China if needed. Domestic freight is fast and cheap. Zero receiving fees regardless of how many suppliers deliver to us.
What if my ecommerce platform is not on your supported list?
We offer API access for custom integrations with any platform. If you prefer not to use an API, you can bulk upload orders using our batch file template. We support CSV, XML, XLS, and other common file formats. We can also accommodate custom file formats if our standard template does not match your system.
