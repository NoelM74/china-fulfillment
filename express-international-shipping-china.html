We are a level 1 agent for HK DHL, HK UPS, and HK FedEx. That means our shipping volumes secure wholesale pricing 30-60% below official carrier list rates. When you ship express through us, you pay what a large enterprise pays, not what a one-off shipper pays.

Express is the right choice when you need a package at your customer's door within a week. Documents, samples, prototypes, high-value products, urgent inventory replenishments, and individual ecommerce orders all ship express. Every shipment includes real-time tracking from pickup in Shenzhen to final delivery, priority customs clearance, and door-to-door service with no additional handling required at either end.

Air freight: the middle ground between speed and cost

Air freight is 40-50% cheaper than express courier but only takes 5-10 days. It is the right choice for Amazon FBA replenishments over 100kg, seasonal inventory builds, and bulk shipments where express is too expensive but ocean is too slow.

We fly cargo on Emirates, Cathay Pacific, China Southern, Singapore Airlines, Ethiopian Airlines, and 20+ additional carriers. Service is airport-to-door: we handle export customs in Shenzhen, air transport, import clearance at destination, and final delivery to your warehouse or Amazon FC. DDP available on all air freight routes.

Minimum shipment weight is 21kg. For Amazon FBA shipments, we create the shipping plan, print Amazon's labels in our warehouse, palletise to Amazon's specification, and ship direct to the designated fulfillment centre. Tracking updates at every stage.

Ocean freight: FCL and LCL from Shenzhen's ports

Yantian and Shekou container terminals are within the city limits of Shenzhen. Our warehouse is minutes from both. That proximity means faster container loading, lower origin charges, and more frequent sailing options than inland-based warehouses that truck containers 3-8 hours to reach a port.

FCL (Full Container Load): 20GP (28 CBM), 40GP (58 CBM), or 40HQ (68 CBM) containers booked on Maersk, MSC, COSCO, CMA CGM, and other tier-1 carriers. Port-to-door service with full customs clearance and inland delivery at destination. Transit to US West Coast: 14-16 days. Transit to Europe: 25-30 days. Transit to SE Asia: 7-10 days.

LCL (Less than Container Load): Share container space with other shippers. Minimum 1 CBM. Weekly departures to major ports worldwide. Your goods are consolidated at our Shenzhen warehouse and loaded into a shared container. You pay only for the volume you use.

Special lines: regional routes and sensitive goods

Special lines are consolidated shipping routes to specific regions. They cost 30-40% less than express courier with similar speed (7-12 days). We operate dedicated lines to the US, UK, Europe, Middle East, and Southeast Asia with weekly departures.

The biggest advantage of special lines is sensitive goods handling. Standard carriers reject many product categories. We ship them via Hong Kong with 3 dedicated flights per week.

Postal packets: budget shipping for lightweight items

For small, lightweight parcels under 2kg, postal services offer the lowest cost per shipment. We ship via ePacket, China Post Air Mail, Hong Kong Post Registered, and Singapore Post Speedpost. Rates start from $3.20/kg. Delivery takes 10-20 days depending on destination and service.

Postal is the right choice for dropshipping sellers on eBay and Etsy, low-value accessories and phone cases, jewellery, and any product where shipping cost needs to stay below $3-5 per order. Tracking ranges from basic acceptance scans (standard post) to full pickup-to-delivery tracking (ePacket and registered mail). Customs clearance is simplified through postal channels with lower inspection rates.

Frequently asked questions

What express courier services do you offer from China?

We ship via DHL, FedEx, UPS, EMS, TNT, and Aramex from our Shenzhen warehouse. As a level 1 agent for HK DHL, HK UPS, and HK FedEx, we offer wholesale rates 30-60% below official carrier pricing. Express delivery takes 2-5 business days to most destinations worldwide.

How much does express shipping from China cost?

Express shipping starts from $8.50 per kilogram via DHL to the US, $9.20/kg to Europe via FedEx, and $10.80/kg to Australia via UPS. Rates depend on weight, dimensions, and destination. We charge by the greater of actual weight or volume weight (L x W x H / 5000 in cm).

What is the difference between express shipping and air freight?

Express shipping (DHL, FedEx, UPS) delivers in 2-5 days with door-to-door service, real-time tracking, and priority customs clearance. It costs from $8.50/kg. Air freight delivers in 5-10 days at $4.50+/kg with a 21kg minimum. Express is better for small urgent shipments. Air freight is better for bulk inventory over 100kg.

Can you ship sensitive goods like batteries, liquids, and supplements?

Yes. We handle batteries (built-in), liquids up to 500ml, non-white powders, food products, supplements, cosmetics, branded goods, and PPE. Sensitive cargo ships via Hong Kong with 3 flights per week to major destinations. We handle all sensitive goods documentation and customs declarations.

What is DDP shipping and should I use it?

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) means all import duties, taxes, and customs clearance fees are paid before the shipment arrives. The recipient receives the package with zero surprise charges. DDP is recommended for D2C ecommerce brands because unexpected duty charges at delivery cause refused packages and negative customer reviews.

How fast is ocean freight from China?

Ocean freight from Shenzhen takes 14-16 days to US West Coast, 25-30 days to Europe, and 7-10 days to Southeast Asia. FCL starts from approximately $1,800 for a 20ft container to the US. LCL starts from 1 CBM minimum with weekly departures from Yantian and Shekou ports.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes. We handle customs documentation, commercial invoices, packing lists, and all required export paperwork for every shipment method. For DDP shipments, we also manage import clearance and duty payment at the destination. Express shipments receive priority customs handling for faster clearance.

Can I combine shipping with your warehouse and fulfillment services?

Yes. If your inventory is stored in our Shenzhen warehouse, shipping is built into the fulfillment workflow. Orders are picked, packed, and handed to the carrier in the same facility. This eliminates the handoff between a separate warehouse and a shipping provider, reducing both cost and transit time.
