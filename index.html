Frequently Asked

Everything sellers ask before making the switch

How much cheaper is your storage than Amazon FBA?
We charge $0.49 per CBM per day — equivalent to $14.70/CBM/month. Amazon charges $30.72/CBM/month from January to September and $84.74/CBM/month in Q4 (October–December). On a 40ft container of stock (58 CBM), you save $20,550 per year. Even sellers with just 10 CBM save over $3,500 annually.
Amazon discontinued FBA Prep in 2026 — can you handle that?
Yes. Since January 1, 2026, Amazon no longer poly-bags, bubble wraps, bundles, kits, or applies FNSKU barcodes for FBA shipments in the US. We handle all of this in our Shenzhen warehouse at $0.99 per carton — every shipment arrives at Amazon fully prepped and compliant with current specifications, including weight limits and Box Content Feed requirements.
Is Shenzhen better than Yiwu for Amazon FBA shipping from China?
Yes, significantly. Shenzhen sits immediately adjacent to Yantian and Shekou ports — two of China's largest container export terminals. Yiwu-based warehouses are 6–8 hours from the nearest comparable port by domestic truck, adding both time and cost to every shipment. For sellers shipping weekly replenishments, that difference accumulates quickly.
What Amazon FBA markets do you ship to?
We ship to all Amazon FBA marketplaces worldwide including the US, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Japan, Canada and Australia, plus 190+ additional countries for DTC fulfilment.
How does the Amazon Seller Central integration work?
Your Amazon product listings sync automatically into your dashboard. When you're ready to replenish, enter the number of cartons — the system creates the shipping plan in Seller Central, downloads labels, has them printed in our warehouse, dispatches the shipment, and uploads tracking numbers automatically. The process that used to take 2 hours takes 2 clicks.
Do my suppliers need to be near Shenzhen?
No. Your suppliers can be anywhere in China — Guangdong, Zhejiang, Jiangsu, Fujian, or further. Domestic freight routes to our warehouse. Delivering to us is often cheaper for suppliers than FOB port delivery, because we handle all export documentation.
Can you handle multiple suppliers consolidating into one shipment?
Yes — no limit on supplier count, zero receiving fees on every inbound delivery. All your suppliers deliver to one Shenzhen address. Purchase orders counted, spec sheets enforced, discrepancies photographed, and outbound moves as one consolidated shipment. What was four freight invoices becomes one.
How quickly can we get operational?
Most new clients are fully operational within 3–5 business days. Account set up, Amazon listings synced, suppliers briefed on the new delivery address. The first 30 days of storage are free — zero cost to run your first complete cycle before committing.
