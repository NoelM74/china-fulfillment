Frequently Asked

Everything brands ask before making the switch

How long does shipping take from your Shenzhen warehouse?
From our Shenzhen warehouse: 6–10 days to the United States, 5–8 days to the UK, and 6–14 days across Europe. Express options are available for faster delivery to key markets. All transit times are tracked end-to-end.
Do you ship DDP — with duties and taxes already included?
Yes. We ship DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) to the US, UK and EU. All import duties, customs fees and local taxes are calculated and covered before your order ships — your customers never receive an unexpected bill at delivery. With current US tariff levels on Chinese goods, we strongly recommend DDP for all US-bound shipments to protect your brand reputation and avoid negative customer experiences.
Can you handle Kickstarter and Indiegogo crowdfunding fulfillment?
Yes — crowdfunding fulfillment is a core specialty. We handle kitting, custom packaging, documentation and carrier selection to ship rewards to backers in 200+ countries. 6–10 days to the US, 5–8 days to the UK. We manage the full logistics so you can focus on your campaign and community.
How does the Shopify integration work?
Your Shopify store connects directly to our WMS. Orders sync automatically the moment they're placed. We pick, pack in your branded packaging, and dispatch — tracking numbers are auto-uploaded back to Shopify and emailed to your customers. The whole process is automated and requires no manual input from your team.
How much cheaper is your storage than Amazon FBA?
We charge $0.49 per CBM per day — equivalent to $14.70/CBM/month. Amazon charges $30.72/CBM/month from January to September and $84.74/CBM/month in Q4 (October–December). On a 40ft container of stock (58 CBM), you save $20,550 per year. Even sellers with just 10 CBM save over $3,500 annually.
Amazon discontinued FBA Prep in 2026 — can you handle that?
Yes. Since January 1, 2026, Amazon no longer poly-bags, bubble wraps, bundles, kits, or applies FNSKU barcodes for FBA shipments in the US. We handle all of this in our Shenzhen warehouse at $0.99 per carton — every shipment arrives at Amazon fully prepped and compliant with current specifications, including weight limits and Box Content Feed requirements.
Do my suppliers need to be near Shenzhen?
No. Your suppliers can be anywhere in China — Guangdong, Zhejiang, Jiangsu, Fujian, or further. Domestic freight routes to our warehouse. Delivering to us is often cheaper for suppliers than FOB port delivery because we handle all export documentation.
How quickly can we get operational?
Most new clients are fully operational within 3–5 business days. Account set up, Shopify connected, suppliers briefed on the new delivery address. The first 30 days of storage are free — zero cost to run your first complete cycle before committing.
